About TorreyMUN

Learn by
doing

TorreyMUN is the biggest club on La Jolla Country Day School's campus. Delegates build leadership, public speaking, diplomatic conduct, and professional writing skills — not by memorizing, but by debating.

2015 → today

Our history

  • 2015 · Founded

    Students Matthew Wirtz and Nikita Nair start the club with teacher Ms. Sara Bivin, attending their first regional conferences.

  • 2020–2026 · Growth years

    Under advisor Dr. Fiona Halloran, the program expands into one of the region's strongest delegations and a six-year attendee of THIMUN in The Hague — the most prestigious MUN conference in the world.

  • 2025 · First standalone fall conference

    TorreyMUN hosts the region's season-opening conference, drawing 250+ delegates from schools across Southern California and Tijuana.

  • 2026 · TorreyMUN VIII

    Now advised by Mr. Jonathan Shulman, the team hosts its eighth session on October 10, 2026.
