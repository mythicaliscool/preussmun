About the Club

Learn diplomacy by doing it.

2015
Year founded
#1
Biggest club on LJCDS's campus
6 yrs
Attending THIMUN in The Hague

Our mission is to build leadership, public speaking, diplomatic conduct, and professional writing skills — the tools delegates carry far beyond the committee room.

We believe you learn through doing. Members represent countries and characters, draft real resolutions, and navigate live crises at conferences across San Diego and around the world.

Every year, our delegation travels internationally — from six consecutive years at THIMUN in The Hague to SSUNS in Montreal, PANAMUN in Panama City, and IMUN in Lisbon.

Before You Debate

Position Paper guidelines.

Every delegate submits one. Here's exactly what we're looking for.

Requirements

  • 1 single-spaced page (excluding sources)
  • 12-pt Times New Roman
  • 1-inch margins
  • No headers, section breaks, or images
  • Chicago-style citations on a separate page

Structure

HDR
Header
Name, School, Committee, and Character Name.
P1
Background
Set the scene — the history and context of your committee's topic.
P2
Your position
Your character's or country's stance on the issue at hand.
P3
Goals & solutions
What you want to achieve in committee — and how you'll get there.
